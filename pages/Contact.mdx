# Contact

A contact page with form and optional location info.

## Semantic Structure

```html
<body>
  <header><!-- Nav --></header>
  <main>
    <article>
      <header>
        <h1>Contact Us</h1>
        <p>Get in touch with our team</p>
      </header>
      <section>
        <form>
          <label>
            <span>Name</span>
            <input type="text" name="name" required />
          </label>
          <label>
            <span>Email</span>
            <input type="email" name="email" required />
          </label>
          <label>
            <span>Message</span>
            <textarea name="message" required></textarea>
          </label>
          <button type="submit">Send Message</button>
        </form>
      </section>
      <aside>
        <address>
          <strong>Email</strong>
          <a href="mailto:hello@acme.com">hello@acme.com</a>
        </address>
        <address>
          <strong>Location</strong>
          <p>San Francisco, CA</p>
        </address>
      </aside>
    </article>
  </main>
  <footer><!-- Footer --></footer>
</body>
```
